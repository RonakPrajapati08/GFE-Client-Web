TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION

We are one of the largest manufacturers of Disc Blades in India. Presently, we are manufacturing different types of Discs i.e. all sizes ranging from 12” inches (304.8mm) to 30’’ inches (762mm) in diameter and 2.5 mm to 12 mm in thickness. Discs blades are manufactured by cross rolling process from high carbon steel & rolled billets of grade SAE 1070, SAE 1080, Chrome Manganese steel & Boron Steel.

High Carbon disc hardness: 38 – 42 HRC
Boron disc hardness: 44 – 48 HRC
Finish: Powder Coating / Paint
